What's inside

Loud, clean, and genuinely smart

Most boosters are five sliders on a screen. SoundLift is built like studio gear — and it listens.

Adaptive AI

Recognizes music, dialog, games and movies from the sound itself, then retunes boost and EQ the moment your content changes. It even learns the level you prefer.

Boost beyond MAX

Up to 200% and beyond with MAX mode, on speakers, wired headphones and Bluetooth. A built-in limiter keeps it loud without crackle or distortion.

12-band studio EQ

31 Hz to 16 kHz with precise steps, DJ-style Bass, Space, Amp and Balance knobs, ambience modes, and sound profiles you can save.

Every device remembered

Your phone speaker, your headphones and each Bluetooth speaker keep their own level — and their own EQ, if you want.

Control from anywhere

Notification controls, a Quick Settings tile and a home screen widget. It keeps working in the background and on the lock screen.

Made for your ears

Sound Check builds a personal listening profile for your headphones, and a daily Safe Score estimates how loud your day sounded — all on your device.

Nothing is recorded. Nothing leaves your phone.

The AI analyses the audio spectrum on your device, in the moment. No audio is ever recorded, stored or transmitted — not to us, not to anyone. Your sound profile and listening history stay on your phone.
