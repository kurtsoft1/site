1. What SoundLift does

SoundLift processes audio playing on your device: it can raise the volume beyond the system limit, apply equalization and other effects, and adjust those settings automatically. It works with the audio your device is already playing; it does not download, copy or distribute any content.

3. Listening safely

SoundLift can make sound much louder than your device normally allows. Loud listening for long periods can damage hearing, and driving small speakers hard for a long time can strain them.

  • Increase the level gradually and take breaks.
  • If you hear distortion or crackling, lower the level.
  • Use the Safe Score and the optional auto step-down feature — they exist to help you.

You are responsible for the volume you choose. SoundLift is a tool; how loud you run it is your decision.
