Data Deletion

App: Booster (com.kurtsoft.booster) · Developer: Kurtsoft Yazılım ve Teknoloji A.Ş., Antalya, Türkiye

1. What data exists

Booster does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. The only data the app keeps is your local settings (boost level, equalizer curves, presets, preferences), stored in the app's private storage on your device only. Nothing is ever transmitted to us or to any third party.

2. How to delete your data

Because all data lives on your device, you can delete it yourself at any time — no request to us is needed:

  • Clear app data: Android Settings > Apps > Booster > Storage & cache > Clear storage. This removes all settings immediately.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling Booster removes the app and all of its data from your device.

3. Data kept after deletion

None. Since no data is stored outside your device, deletion is complete and immediate once you clear the app's storage or uninstall it.
